If you do not agree with any part of these terms, please refrain from using our services. We maintain a space of kindness, ecological integrity, and mutual growth — just like a well-tended garden.

“Green & Red Promise” — Our workshops, designs, and interactions are founded on respect for nature and each other. Any violation of this ethos may lead to restricted access.

2. Workshop & Event Policies

Registration & Payment

All workshop fees, garden tours, and ikebana intensives must be paid in full at the time of registration unless a deposit option is explicitly stated. We accept credit cards, PayPal, and other methods listed at checkout. Prices are listed in USD and do not include optional supplies unless noted.

Cancellations & Refunds

  • By participant: Cancellations made more than 7 days before the workshop date receive a full refund (minus a 5% processing fee). Cancellations within 3–7 days receive 50% credit toward a future workshop. Cancellations less than 48 hours before the event are non-refundable, but you may transfer your spot to a friend (just notify us!).
  • By Yugen Flora: If we need to cancel due to extreme weather, instructor illness, or low enrollment, you will receive a full refund or the option to transfer to a future session with a 15% discount code as a thank-you for your flexibility.

In-Person Garden Etiquette

We ask all guests to respect the plants, stay on designated paths, avoid picking flowers or leaves unless guided by an instructor, and treat fellow attendees with care. We are a scent-sensitive and eco-friendly space — please refrain from strong perfumes or disruptive behavior.

3. Products & E-Commerce Terms

Our online shop (plants, handmade pottery, seeds, curated tools) is available while supplies last. We strive to represent colors and sizes accurately, but due to natural variations in botanicals, slight differences may occur — this is part of the beauty of living art.

  • Shipping: We ship live plants only within the continental US during mild weather windows (spring/fall). Substitutions may be needed for plant health; if a specific variety is unavailable, we will contact you. Seeds and dry goods ship worldwide except where restricted.
  • Returns & Exchanges: If a plant arrives damaged or unhealthy, please send a photo within 48 hours of delivery and we will issue a replacement or store credit. Non-plant items may be returned unopened within 14 days for a refund minus original shipping costs.
  • Pick-up orders: From our Portland garden, we hold orders for 7 days. Unclaimed orders become donations to our community compost program.
