Our Gardening Philosophy

We believe gardening is a dialogue between human intention and nature's wild beauty. Our green & red theme celebrates life (green) and passion (red) — from emerald ferns to crimson maples, from basil to begonias. Every leaf, every petal tells a story.

Essential Gardening Wisdom

Soil First

Rich, loamy soil with compost feeds your green friends. Red wigglers are a bonus!

Water Deeply

Infrequent deep watering beats daily sprinkles — encourages deep roots.

Observe Light

Red-leaved plants need more sun to maintain color. Green plants vary.

Prune with Purpose

Cut dead, diseased, or crossing branches. Late winter is prime time.

Natural Pest Control

Nasturtiums (red/orange) and marigolds deter pests organically.

Seasonal Rhythm

Plant cool greens (spinach, kale) in spring/fall. Red peppers love summer heat.
