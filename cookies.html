Cookies & Digital Petals

Small data tokens that help our garden thrive — we use them responsibly, with clarity and care.

Last revised: April 2025

🍪 Our gentle use of cookies

Just as a garden benefits from subtle seasonal cues, our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors explore our botanical world. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can manage them. At Yugen Flora Workshop, transparency blooms naturally.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device over time, retain certain settings, and collect anonymous usage data. We also use local storage and similar technologies for functional purposes. No cookie should ever contain personal information unless you have voluntarily provided it (e.g., by filling out a form).

2. Types of cookies we use

We only deploy cookies that are essential, functional, or analytical — never intrusive or for unsolicited advertising. Below is a transparent breakdown:

Cookie CategoryPurposeDurationExamples
Strictly NecessaryEnsure core functionality: navigation, security, form submissions, and cookie consent memory. Without these, the site would not perform properly.Session / Persistent (max 1 year)session_id, csrf_token, cookie_preferences_set
Functional / PreferenceRemember your choices like language, region, or whether you've dismissed the workshop notification banner.Up to 12 monthslanguage_pref, hide_workshop_popup
Analytical (Performance)Collect anonymized data about visitor behavior (pages visited, time spent, referral sources) to improve our digital presence. We use first-party analytics; no cross-site tracking.Persistent (up to 14 months)_ga, _gid (anonymized with IP masking)

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels from third-party networks, or any cookies for behavioral profiling across different websites.

3. How we obtain your consent

When you first visit our website, a subtle cookie banner appears, allowing you to accept or manage non-essential cookies. By continuing to browse after clicking "Accept Functional & Analytics" or adjusting your preferences, you grant us permission to place the selected cookies. You may change your mind at any time using the “Cookie Preferences” button below or your browser settings. Essential cookies are always active to guarantee a secure experience.

Your choice matters. We respect your autonomy. You can disable non-essential cookies via the button below. However, disabling analytics cookies may prevent us from improving our services.

4. Third-party services & embedded content

Occasionally we embed content from trusted partners (e.g., a calendly link for workshop booking, or Instagram posts). These third-party services may set their own cookies if you interact with them. We do not control those cookies, and we encourage you to review their respective policies. However, we only integrate partners committed to privacy and we never share your personal data without explicit consent.

5. Managing & deleting cookies

You have full control. Here are the ways to manage cookies on our website and beyond:

  • 🔧 Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete all cookies, including essential ones (though this may break certain website features). Check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • 🍪 Our preference center: Click the "Customize your cookie preferences" button above or below — it will open an interactive panel where you can toggle analytics and functional cookies on/off.
  • ⚙️ Global privacy controls: If your browser sends a Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal, we will automatically respect it and disable non-essential cookies.

To disable Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can also install the official Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Cookies & personal data

Our cookies do not directly store sensitive personal information (like your name or address). However, some identifiers (e.g., session ID) might be linked to your account if you are logged into our workshop portal. In that case, the same privacy protections described in our Privacy Policy apply. We never use cookies to build profiling for marketing outside Yugen Flora Workshop.

7. Updates to this cookies policy

As our digital garden evolves, we may revisit this policy to reflect new technologies or regulations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. If we make a material change that affects cookie functionality or consent, we will notify you via a website banner.
